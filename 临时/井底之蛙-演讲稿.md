# The Frog in the Well — Speech Script

Hello everyone. Today I want to tell you a Chinese story. It is called "The Frog in the Well."

A long time ago, there was a little frog. He lived in a well. Every day he looked up and saw a small blue sky. He was very happy. He said, "I am the king of the world! The world is small and I can see it all!"

One day, a bird came to the well. The bird said, "Hello, little frog! Come out and see the big world!" The frog was surprised. "The world is not big," he said. "It is very small!"

The bird smiled. "No, no," said the bird. "The world is so big! There are big blue seas. There are tall green trees. Come and see!"

So the frog jumped out of the well. He looked around. Oh! The sky was so big! He saw green trees. He saw a beautiful lake. He was very surprised.

The frog said, "Now I know. The world is very big. My well was too small. I could only see a small part of the world."

The lesson is: do not be a frog in the well. Open your eyes. See the big beautiful world. Learn new things every day.

Thank you!
